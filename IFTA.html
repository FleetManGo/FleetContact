Penalties for Missing IFTA Sticker

If a semi-truck does not have the IFTA sticker or it is missing, the penalties can include:

  • Fines: Fines for not having the decal can vary depending on the state or province, but they can range from a flat fine to daily fines until the vehicle complies.
  • Inability to operate: In some jurisdictions, a truck can be temporarily prohibited from operating across state or provincial lines until the proper stickers are affixed.
  • Back Taxes: The truck operator may have to pay taxes retroactively for the time the vehicle was operating without the necessary decals, along with penalties and interest for non-compliance.
